IBM Presence Insights (Ongoing)

A product/platform which will enable people working "on the floor" (in retail, fulfillment centres, museums, airports, etc.) to get a deeper sense of the world around them — PI is comprised of a set of tools people can utilize to weave together physical and digital experiences/systems in an intuitive and beautiful manner. I'm heading up the design of the PI's info–architecture, interaction models, and method of physical–digital infrastructure setup. View the product or read my ongoing project notes.

IBM RapidApps

An idealistic product/platform built to “Democratize the process of application development”. An attempt at empowering (and educating) non-technical people to build applications to solve everyday life/business problems. I designed and utilized “spatially-concrete” interface/interaction models so that abstract software development concepts were made easier to understand, and led the design of RA's Rules/Logic composing interface. Spoiler alert: The product ended up failing. Read a long-form postmortem on this project.

Mobility and Catheter

With a small and independently-funded team, conducted research towards a holistic redesign of the “catheter management experience”, focusing on ease of use, cleanliness, reduction of UTI, accessibility, discreetness, and a user’s dignity. Led the design of the physical product and user experience. Read my case study.
