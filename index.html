Some work in brief —

IBM Presence Insights

A product/platform which will enable people working "on the floor" (in retail, fulfillment centres, museums, airports, etc.) to get a deeper sense of the world around them. Comprised of a set of tools which enable people to weave together physical and digital experiences/systems in an intuitive and beautiful manner. View the product or read my case study.

IBM RapidApps

A product/platform built to “Democratize the process of application development”. An attempt at empowering (and educating) ordinary/non-technical people to build applications to solve everyday life/business problems. RapidApps utilized “spatially-concrete” interface design metaphors so that abstract software development concepts were made easier to understand. View the beta product or read my case study.

Mobility and Catheter

With a team of three others, conducted research towards a holistic redesign of the “catheter management experience”, focusing on ease of use, cleanliness, reduction of UTI, accessibility, discreetness, and a user’s dignity. Led the design of the physical product and user experience. View the beta product or read my case study.

Smart Design, OXO

Worked the role of an industrial designer building prototypes, conducting research, producing visuals for various projects, and assisted in the development of several products. Co-led the development of various OXO products, a few of which are in now in production. Gained considerable insight into the design of both soft and hard goods and the design of ergonomic tools.
