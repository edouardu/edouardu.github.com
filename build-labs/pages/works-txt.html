*Simple Tutorial Site*
2015 While I was at IBM, I made it a personal mission to teach designers that Git(hub) was the shit — both the technology and the popular platform. An often repeated question many had was, “How do I make and host web-based prototypes?” This [website](http://labs.edouard.us/how2pages) I spun up in less than 2 minutes was the answer I would give, and later attach to a Slack shortcut. *Text Adventure Game*
2014 I had an incredibly wacky idea a few years ago to make my website/résumé into an interactive text adventure. The idea was that you'd chat to a fictional version of me and learn about my work/ethos/whatever in a conversational tone. You could enact violence upon me to end the experience. This whole thing was built in [Twine](https://twinery.org/) and heavily inspired my one of my biggest inspirations/mentors, [Anna Anthropy](http://auntiepixelante.com/). [Source is here](http://labs.edouard.us/resume/). ###### — **Snapshots**
*Raspberry Pi Installation*
2014 A project inspired by a currently-defunct undertaking by [Jefferey Scudder](http://www.rey.sc/). The original project comprised of 3-ish second long webcam videos taken the same time every day. What resulted was a string of youtube videos depicting life as it passed by in front of a computer. I thought it would be cool to mimic this project with a remotely-controlled Raspberry Pi and camera set to go off at 11am and 11pm every day with a simple cron job script. [Right now my project is also defunct](https://github.com/edouerd/ss), but I plan on reinstating it soon. {% endblock body %} {% endfilter %}
