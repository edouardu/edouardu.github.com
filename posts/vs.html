What does it mean?

It couldn't be this, right?

The Virtual Studio project was seen as a possible solution to a situation that IBM had been dealing with for a long time — how can remote collaboration and levelsetting on projects be made better?

Most people at IBM will tell you straight up that our communication and collaboration platforms are leaving much to be desired. The archival and retreival of crucial project artifacts and documentation as of early 2015 is as rough around the edges as it can get.
